# Nexus Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name**: Nexus  
**Tagline**: Connecting Capital on Stacks  
**Mission**: To seamlessly connect lenders and borrowers in a trustless, transparent DeFi environment

## Brand Voice

- **Professional** yet approachable
- **Transparent** - no hidden terms
- **Connected** - emphasizing the network effect
- **Secure** - built on Clarity's predictability
- **Empowering** - giving users control of their capital

## Key Messages

1. "Connect your capital to opportunities"
2. "Transparent lending, powered by Clarity"
3. "Earn yield while you sleep"
4. "Borrow without selling"
5. "The nexus of DeFi on Stacks"

## Color Palette Suggestions

- **Primary**: Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) - Trust, stability
- **Secondary**: Bright Cyan (#06B6D4) - Connection, flow
- **Accent**: Orange (#F97316) - Energy, yield
- **Background**: Dark (#0F172A) / Light (#F8FAFC)

## Logo Concepts

1. **Connected Nodes**: Circles connected by lines forming a network
2. **Infinity Loop**: Representing continuous flow of capital
3. **Bridge Symbol**: Connecting lenders and borrowers
4. **Hexagon Network**: Modern, tech-focused geometric pattern
